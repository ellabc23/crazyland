Coaster of Love

When you enter the winding line of the Coaster of Love, you will see many paper hearts that our staff remake every day. You will have a choice of seating. Each cubicle seats two and the pink squishy seats will make you want to ride time and time again. The beginning of the ride is a giant hill. Then the real fun begins. Ups! Downs! Two corkscrews and three loops until you move up, down, up, and down again. A heart! You will blast through that heart. The ride time is four minutes.
